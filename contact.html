Common Questions

Do I need to create an account to use the tools?

No. All tools on Squoosh.online are available without registration. You can start compressing images, converting videos, or optimizing PDFs immediately without providing any personal information.

Are my files uploaded to your servers?

For our native browser-based tools, absolutely not. All processing happens locally on your device using WebAssembly technology. Your files never leave your computer. When we link to third-party tools, those services have their own processing methods and privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.

Is Squoosh.online really free?

Yes, completely free. We do not offer premium tiers, paid subscriptions, or hidden charges. Our platform is supported by advertising, which allows us to keep all tools and features accessible to everyone at no cost.

Can I suggest a new tool or feature?

Absolutely! We love hearing from our community. Use the contact form above or send an email to [email protected] with your suggestion. We review every piece of feedback and prioritize features based on community demand.

What browsers are supported?

Squoosh.online works on all modern browsers including Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, Microsoft Edge, Apple Safari, and their mobile versions. We recommend keeping your browser updated to the latest version for the best experience and full WebAssembly support.
