About Us

About Squoosh.online

We believe everyone deserves access to powerful, privacy-respecting file optimization tools — without paying a dime or compromising their data.

Our Mission

At Squoosh.online, our mission is to democratize file optimization for every person on the internet. Whether you are a professional photographer looking to compress high-resolution images without visible quality loss, a small business owner needing to shrink PDF invoices for email delivery, or a content creator preparing videos for social media platforms, we want to ensure that the tools you need are always within reach — free of charge, free of registration barriers, and free of privacy concerns.

The modern web demands speed. Search engines rank faster-loading pages higher. Visitors bounce from sites that take more than a few seconds to render. Email providers reject attachments that exceed size limits. In every corner of digital life, file size matters. Yet the tools that help people optimize their files have historically been locked behind expensive software licenses, complicated desktop installations, or cloud-based services that require you to upload sensitive documents to unknown servers. We set out to change that equation entirely.

Our approach combines two strategies. First, we build and maintain browser-based tools that leverage cutting-edge WebAssembly technology to process your files directly on your own device. Your images, documents, and media never leave your computer. Second, for tasks that require more specialized processing, we curate and recommend only the most trustworthy, highest-quality third-party tools available online. Every recommendation we make is tested, reviewed, and monitored to ensure it meets our standards for quality, privacy, and reliability.

Our Story

Squoosh.online was born out of frustration. Like many people who work with digital media daily, our founding team spent years wrestling with the same recurring problems: bloated image files slowing down websites, PDFs too large for email attachments, and video files that took forever to upload. The solutions available at the time were fragmented and often unsatisfactory. Desktop software like Adobe Photoshop could optimize images beautifully, but it cost hundreds of dollars a year and required significant technical knowledge. Online compression tools were free but required uploading your files to remote servers, raising serious privacy and security concerns, especially for business documents, personal photos, and confidential materials.

We asked ourselves a simple question: why can't file optimization happen right inside the browser? With the maturation of WebAssembly, a technology that allows near-native performance inside web browsers, we realized the answer had finally arrived. We could build powerful compression and conversion tools that run entirely on the user's device, offering the speed and quality of desktop software with the convenience and accessibility of a website. No downloads, no installations, no server uploads, no accounts required.

That vision became Squoosh.online. We launched with a handful of image optimization tools and quickly expanded to cover video compression, PDF manipulation, and a wide range of file conversion utilities. Along the way, we recognized that no single platform could do everything perfectly, so we began curating a directory of the best external tools for specialized tasks. Today, Squoosh.online serves as both a powerful toolbox and a trusted guide, helping hundreds of thousands of users around the world optimize their digital files safely, quickly, and for free.

Our Technology

At the heart of Squoosh.online lies WebAssembly (often abbreviated as Wasm), a revolutionary technology that allows high-performance code to run directly inside your web browser. Traditionally, tasks like image compression, video transcoding, and PDF manipulation required either dedicated desktop software or powerful remote servers. WebAssembly changes that by providing a compact binary format that runs at near-native speed within the browser's sandboxed environment. This means we can offer you the same quality of file optimization that expensive desktop applications provide, but through a simple web page you can access from any device.

The privacy implications of this architecture are profound. When you use one of our native WebAssembly tools to compress an image or convert a document, the entire operation happens locally on your machine. Your file is read from your local storage, processed by the WebAssembly module running in your browser, and the optimized result is saved back to your device. At no point during this process does your file travel across the internet. No data is sent to our servers or any third-party servers. This eliminates the risk of data interception during transit, unauthorized access on remote servers, or retention of your files by service providers.

We continuously invest in improving our WebAssembly toolchain to support new file formats, better compression algorithms, and faster processing speeds. Our engineering team monitors the latest developments in browser technology to ensure compatibility across all major platforms, including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. Whether you are on a desktop computer, a laptop, a tablet, or a smartphone, our tools are designed to deliver consistent, high-quality results with the performance and privacy you deserve.
