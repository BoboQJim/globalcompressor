Our Mission

Squoosh.online was founded with a single goal: To democratize high-end file optimization.

We believe that a faster web is a better web. By providing professional-grade tools that run directly in your browser using WebAssembly technology, we eliminate the need for expensive software and risky file uploads to unknown servers.

Our portal serves as a curated directory, connecting users with both our native high-speed tools and industry-recognized partners like TinyPNG and SmallPDF, ensuring you always have the best tool for the job.

Privacy & Data Policy

Your Privacy is our Core Value.

  • Local Processing: Our native tools (Squoosh Pro, Image Stitch, Video Optimize) process files locally in your browser's memory. Your data never touches our servers.
  • No File Storage: We do not store, view, or copy any files you process.
  • Third-Party Tools: When using external partner tools (e.g., TinyPNG), your data is subject to their respective privacy policies. We only partner with industry leaders who maintain high security standards.
  • Analytics: We use minimal analytics (Firebase/Clarity) to improve site performance. This data is aggregated and anonymous.
